Ingredients:
    - 1.5 lbs chicken breast
    - 2 cloves garlic
    - 1 shallot
    - 4 large peaches, sliced
    - quarter cup balsamic vinegar
    - third cup torn fresh basil
    - pepper, salt, olive oil

Steps:
    1. Heat olive oil in skillet.
    2. Season chicken with salt & pepper. Cook in oil and set aside.
    3. Add minced garlic and chopped shallots to pan. Saut√© for 2 minutes.
    4. Cook peaches until soft. Approximately 5 minutes.
    5. Add balsamic vinegar and cook for 2 minutes more, until reduced.
    6. Stir in basil.
    7. Serve chicken with balsamic peaches.
