Ingredients:
    - 7oz (about half of a can) coconut milk
    - 1 tbsp honey
    - 1 large clove of minced garlic
    - 1 tbsp minced ginger
    - 1 tsp soy sauce
    - cinammon, curry powder, salt, pepper (to taste)
    - 2 lbs chicken breast

Steps:
    1. Mix everything but chicken breast in a bowl.
    2. Add chicken breast and let marinate.
    3. Grill.
