Ingredients:
    - 2 lbs ground turkey
	- 3 egg whites
    - 1 cup quick cooking oats
    - 0.5 tsp cumin, thyme
    - 1 tsp salt
    - 2 tsp dry yellow mustard, black pepper, chipotle pepper spice
    - 2 tbsp garlic, minced
    - 1 small white onion, chopped
    - 2 celery stalks, chopped

Steps:
    1. Heat oven to 375ÂºF
    2. Chop onion and celery stalks. Mince garlic.
    3. Mix ingredients in bowl.
    4. Spray muffin tin with oil.
    5. Form balls and put in muffin slots. Should fill all 12 muffin holes.
    6. Bake for 35 minutes.
