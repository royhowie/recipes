Ingredients:
    - 4.5 cups flower
    - ⅔ cup milk
    - 2 eggs
    - 1 can (15oz) pumpkin purée
    - 2 packets (¼ oz each) instant yeast 
    - 2 tbsp honey
    - 2 tsp nutmeg
    - 2 tsp cinammon
    - 4 tbsp butter
    - salt (to taste)
    - melted butter mixed with honey for brushing & dipping

Steps:
    1. Add milk and butter to bowl; microwave for 45s. Stir until butter melts.
    2. Mix in 2 eggs, 1 can of pumpkin purée, 2 tbsp honey. Set aside.
    3. In large bowl, mix the flower, yeast, cinammon, and nutmeg.
    4. Microwave pumpkin mixture for 15s then pour into large bowl.
    5. Knead the dough.
    6. Let rise for an hour or until dough has doubled in size.
    7. Grease sheet. Divide dough into 16 pieces and place onto sheet.
    8. Brush with honey-butter mixture.
    9. Bake for 15 minutes or until golden brown.

Note:
    If there is any leftover melted butter and honey, spread it on the the
    freshly baked bread. Mind-blowingly scrumptious.
