Ingredients:
    - 1 cup Grey Poupon Dijon Mustard (country or regular)
    - 1 cup Nellie & Joe's Famous Key West Lime Juice
    - 1 cup olive oil
    - 1 tablespoon chopped garlic 
    - 2 lbs chicken breast

Steps:
    1. Mix mustard, lime juice, olive oil, and garlic in a bowl.
    2. Add chicken breast and let marinate for some time.
    3. Grill.
