Ingredients:
    - 1 cup mashed ripe bananas (2-3 bananas)
    - 1 cup flour (optional: whole wheat)
    - ½ cup peanut butter
    - ½ cup (Greek) yogurt
    - ¼ cup honey
    - 2 eggs
    - 3 tbsp olive oil
    - 2 tsp vanilla extract
    - 1 tsp baking powder
    - ¼ tsp baking soda
    - ½ tsp salt
    - ½ tsp cinammon

Steps:
    1. Heat oven to 400ºF. Lightly grease 12-cup muffin tin.
    2. Mash the bananas in a bowl.
    3. Stir in the peanut butter, honey, yogurt, eggs, oil, and vanilla.
    4. Add flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon. Mix.
    5. Bake for about 25 minutes.
